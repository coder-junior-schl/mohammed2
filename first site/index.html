Welcome to Loving Homes

Welcome to Loving Homes, the premier dog hotel in Hong Kong. Our goal is to provide a safe, comfortable and enjoyable environment for dogs while their owners are away. We understand that pets are part of the family, which is why we treat every dog that stays with us with care, patience and attention.

At Loving Homes, we focus on creating an environment where dogs can feel relaxed and happy. Our trained staff members supervise all activities and make sure every dog receives the exercise, rest and care they need throughout their stay.

Our Services

We provide a wide range of services designed to keep dogs active, healthy and entertained. Some of the main services we offer include:

  • Comfortable sleeping areas designed for dogs of different sizes
  • Daily outdoor walks and supervised playtime
  • Professional grooming services including washing and trimming
  • Healthy feeding schedules based on each dog's needs
  • 24/7 supervision by trained staff members

Modern Facilities

Our facilities are designed to give dogs enough space to move freely and enjoy their stay. We provide both indoor and outdoor areas where dogs can relax, play and socialize safely. Each area is regularly cleaned and maintained to ensure the highest level of hygiene and comfort.

Flexible Packages

Every dog has different needs, which is why we offer several packages that owners can choose from. Whether your dog needs a short stay, a full day of activities, or grooming services, our packages are designed to give pets the best possible experience.

At Loving Homes, we believe that a happy dog means a happy owner. Our team works hard to ensure every dog receives the care, attention and love they deserve.
