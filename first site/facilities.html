Grooming Spa

Professional grooming and pampering to keep your dog looking its best.

Our grooming spa provides washing, brushing, trimming and nail care services performed by trained staff. We ensure that every dog receives gentle care and attention so they feel relaxed and comfortable during the grooming process.

Forest Walks

Take your dog for scenic walks through safe and supervised forest trails.

Our daily forest walks allow dogs to explore nature, exercise their muscles and enjoy fresh air. Each walk is supervised by experienced staff members to make sure all dogs remain safe while having fun.

Private Grass Area

Spacious and secure outdoor area for play and exercise.

The private grass area gives dogs a large open space where they can run freely, play with toys and interact with other dogs. The area is fully secured and regularly cleaned to maintain a safe and healthy environment for every pet staying at Loving Homes.
